How to play

Every Tuesday for 4 weeks, try one new item from that week's list. Tap what you tried, hit Save, and your points show up on the leaderboard for everyone to see.

Scoring

  • Standard fruit or vegetable — 10 points
  • Chinese-origin bonus item — 20 points
  • Try at least one item all 4 weeks — +15 streak bonus

Bonus items 20 pts each

These items have Chinese origins — a celebration of our company's roots.
